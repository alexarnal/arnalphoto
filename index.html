ABOUT US


Memories Captured in Photo & Video


We offer creative solutions for the El Paso region. It will be an honor to photograph your next wedding, quinceañera, family or corporate event, or graduation celebration. Talk to us about your project; with more than 70 years in the business, we are sure to find a solution.


Arnal Studio was born in Ciudad Juarez in 1946. The founder, Eugenio Arnal, set forth a tradition continued in El Paso by Alfonso Arnal and now Alexandro Arnal. The studio has produced some of the most beautiful photographs in the region, created graphics for Fortune 500 companies, and restored photographic treasures throughout its existence. We continue to reimagine the studio's role in its community by adapting to the needs of our clients. We hope that Arnal Studio will continue being a staple of the El Paso region.
